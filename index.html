Research SIMER: Know about our programs, values, and achievements. Show genuine interest in why we're right for you.

Prepare Your Story: Have a clear narrative about your background, goals, and why you chose management education.

Current Affairs: Stay updated with recent news in business, technology, and current events. Be ready to discuss them briefly.

Mock Interviews: Practice with friends or mentors. Get comfortable speaking confidently about your experiences.

Be Authentic: Don't try to be someone you're not. Interviewers appreciate genuine answers over rehearsed responses.

Listen Carefully: Take time to understand the question before answering. It's okay to pause and think.

Maintain Eye Contact: Show confidence and engagement by looking at the interviewer while speaking.

Use Examples: Back your statements with real examples from your experience or academic projects.

Ask Questions: At the end, ask thoughtful questions about the program or campus life. It shows your interest.

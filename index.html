We Answer Your Concerns

Complete admission takes 1-1.5 hours: 20 minutes written test, 10-15 minutes interview. Same day or next day results!

Bring: 10th and 12th certificates, graduation certificate, entrance exam score card, admission form, and valid ID proof.

You will get results same day evening or next day via call or SMS. We value your time!

Test is just one part! Your interview, personality, and overall impression matter equally. A strong interview can change the outcome.

Sometimes aspirations don't align – and that's okay! We evaluate your readiness for PGDM. If there's a mismatch, a different institution might be better. Don't lose hope – your success awaits!

No worries! We value how you communicate ideas, not perfect grammar. Speak in Hindi and English mix. We'll train you in English during the program!

Grades are just numbers. We care about your attitude, eagerness to learn, and determination. We build real skills and shape professionals!

Dress formally: men wear shirt, trousers, and tie; women wear saree or professional dress. Be well-groomed – first impressions matter!

Be yourself, answer thoughtfully, show genuine interest in your career, and demonstrate commitment to learning. Confidence and honesty matter!

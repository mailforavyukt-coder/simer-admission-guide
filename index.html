COMMON QUESTIONS

We Answer Your Concerns

How long is the entire admission process? ▼

Complete admission takes 1-1.5 hours: 20 minutes written test, 10-15 minutes interview, and quick admin work. Same day or next day results!

What documents should I carry on admission day? ▼

Bring: 10th & 12th certificates, graduation certificate, entrance exam score card, completed admission form, and valid ID proof.

When will I know the result? ▼

You'll get results same day evening or next day via call/SMS. No long waiting – we value your time.

What if I don't do well in the written test? ▼

Test is just one part! Your interview, personality, and overall impression matter equally. A strong interview can completely change the outcome.

What if I get rejected? ▼

We evaluate your placement potential and readiness for PGDM. If you're not selected now, we may give you another chance with more preparation. We believe in you!

How can I get selected? ▼

Be yourself, answer thoughtfully, show genuine interest in your career, and demonstrate commitment to learning. Confidence and honesty go a long way!

My academic grades are not great – does it matter? ▼

Grades are just numbers. We care about your attitude, eagerness to learn, and determination to succeed. Show us your hunger for excellence!

What should I wear? ▼

Dress formally: men wear shirt, trousers, and tie; women wear saree or professional dress. Be well-groomed – first impressions matter!

Can I postpone my admission? ▼

Contact us at 9826107555 to discuss deferment. We'll do our best to accommodate your needs based on availability.

Need more help? ▼

Call us at 9826107555 or visit www.simer.in. Our admissions team is always ready to guide and support you!
